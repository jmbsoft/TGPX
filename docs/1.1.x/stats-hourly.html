Stats - Hourly
Table of Contents
Overview
This interface will show you all of the trades that are in your database with an overview of their hourly stats from the past 24 hours. From this interface you can also edit, disable, reset, and perform other actions on your trade accounts.

Each column represents one hour from the past 24 hours, and contains 3 statistics:
    Raw Ins
    Raw Outs
    Raw Clicks
Toolbar Icons
The toolbar at the bottom of this interface are the same as those in the Overall Stats interface.
