Overview
This interface will show you all of the trades that are in your database with an overview of their stats from the past 60 minutes and 24 hours. From this interface you can also edit, disable, reset, and perform other actions on your trade accounts. The following columns are displayed in the overall stats table:

    Trade - The domain of the trade and an icon you can use to access the menu for the trade. Hover over the icon to access the menu. Click on the domain to visit the trade.
60 Minutes
    In - Raw ins from the past 60 minutes
    Out - Raw outs from the past 60 minutes
    Clks - Raw clicks from the past 60 minutes
    Qual - Ins quality from the past 60 minutes
    Prod - Productivity (clicks/ins) from the past 60 minutes
    Ret - Return (outs/ins) from the past 60 minutes
24 Hours
    In - Raw ins from the past 24 hours
    Out - Raw outs from the past 24 hours
    Clks - Raw clicks from the past 24 hours
    Qual - Ins quality from the past 24 hours
    U.In - Unique ins from the past 24 hours
    Prod - Productivity (clicks/ins) from the past 24 hours
    Skim - Skim (clicks to content/clicks) from the past 24 hours
    Ret - Return (outs/ins) from the past 24 hours
Toolbar Icons
The toolbar at the bottom of this interface has a number of icons you can use to perform different actions. Those are as follows:
    Click this icon to add a new trade. This will display a dialog where you can enter the settings for the new trade.
    Click this icon to reload the current page.
    Click this icon to disable several of your trades at once. Use the checkboxes to select the trades you want to disable.
    Click this icon to enable several of your trades at once. Use the checkboxes to select the trades you want to enable.
    Click this icon to e-mail several of your trades at once. Use the checkboxes to select the trades you want to e-mail. A dialog will appear where you will be able to enter the e-mail message
    Click this icon to edit several of your trades at once. Use the checkboxes to select the trades you want to edit. A dialog will appear allowing you to select the settings you want to update and enter their new values
    Click this icon to reset the stats for several of your trades at once. Use the checkboxes to select the trades you want to reset.
    Click this icon to delete several of your trades at once. Use the checkboxes to select the trades you want to delete. A dialog will appear so you can confirm the deletions and also decide if you want to blacklist the trades.
